The Foryoueats community is where home cooks share what they're making. You can browse thousands of recipes from people all over the world, filter by time constraints, sort in the way that's most useful to you, and save anything you like to your own library. Every recipe you find here was created by someone cooking at home, just like you.

## Browsing and filtering community recipes

Open **Community** from the main navigation to start browsing. You'll see community recipes ordered by newest first by default. Use the sort and filter controls to narrow things down.

### Sort options

| Sort option | What it does |
|---|---|
| Newest | Shows the most recently added recipes first |
| Oldest | Shows the earliest recipes first |
| Last updated | Prioritizes recipes that have been recently edited |
| Name | Alphabetical order by recipe name |
| Prep time | Shortest preparation time first |
| Cook time | Shortest cooking time first |
| Total time | Shortest total time (prep + cook) first |

### Filter by time

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Under 20 minutes" icon="clock">
    Filter to recipes where the selected time (prep, cook, or total) is under 20 minutes. Good for weeknight meals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Over 20 minutes" icon="hourglass">
    Filter to recipes that take longer — useful when you have more time and want a more involved cook.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Combine sort and filter to get the most relevant results. For example, sort by **Prep time** and filter to **Under 20 minutes** to find the quickest dishes to get on the table.
</Tip>

## Saving community recipes

When you find a recipe you want to keep, tap **Save to library**. The recipe is added to your recipe library, where you can:

- Add it to a cookbook for easy access later
- Include it in your meal plan
- Add its ingredients to a shopping list
- Use it in pantry matching

<Note>
  Saving a community recipe creates a copy in your library. If the original author edits or deletes their recipe, your saved copy is not affected.
</Note>

## Controlling your recipe privacy

Every recipe you create in Foryoueats has its own privacy setting. You choose who can see it, and you can change that setting at any time.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Private" icon="lock">
    Only you can see and access this recipe. Use this while you're still developing a dish or for recipes you don't want to share.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Friends" icon="users">
    Visible to the people you follow on Foryoueats. Good for sharing with family or a trusted group.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community" icon="globe">
    Published publicly. Anyone browsing the community can find, view, and save your recipe.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### How to change a recipe's privacy setting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recipe">
    Go to your recipe library and open the recipe you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open recipe settings">
    Tap the settings or edit icon on the recipe page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the privacy level">
    Select **Private**, **Friends**, or **Community** from the sharing options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the change">
    Confirm the update. The new setting takes effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  When you change a recipe from Community to Private, it is removed from community browsing right away. Anyone who already saved the recipe to their own library keeps their copy.
</Warning>
