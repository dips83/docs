Shopping lists in Foryoueats connect directly to your recipes and meal plans, so you spend less time writing down ingredients and more time actually cooking. You can create as many lists as you need, collaborate with others in real time, and check items off right from your phone while you're in the store.

## What you can do with shopping lists

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Unlimited lists, always free" icon="list">
    Create as many shopping lists as you want — one per week, one per store, or one per household member. There's no limit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add from recipes in one tap" icon="plus">
    Open any recipe and tap **Add to shopping list**. All ingredients are added instantly to the list of your choice.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collaborate with Pantry Pals" icon="users">
    Invite people to any list as Pantry Pals. They can view, add items, and check things off in real time alongside you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Check off items while you shop" icon="square-check">
    Tap items as you add them to your cart. Checked items move to the bottom of the list so you always know what's left.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to create a shopping list

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Shopping Lists">
    Open the **Shopping Lists** section from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new list">
    Tap **New list** and give it a name — for example, "This week's groceries" or "Weekend baking run."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add items">
    Type items in manually, or add ingredients directly from a recipe (see below). You can combine both methods on the same list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with Pantry Pals (optional)">
    Tap **Share** and enter the email addresses or usernames of the people you want to add. They'll get access to view and edit the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shop and check items off">
    Open the list on your phone while you shop. Tap each item as you pick it up. Checked items move to the bottom automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding ingredients from a recipe

You can add ingredients to a shopping list from any recipe page — your own recipes and community recipes both work the same way.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recipe">
    Find the recipe you want to cook and open its detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add to shopping list">
    Tap the **Add to shopping list** button near the ingredients section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your list">
    Select an existing list or create a new one. All ingredients from the recipe are added immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you're cooking a recipe for a different number of servings, adjust the serving size on the recipe page first. The adjusted quantities will carry over to your shopping list.
</Tip>

## Generating a list from your meal plan

If you've built a meal plan for the week, you can generate a complete shopping list from it in one action — no need to add recipes one by one.

1. Open your **Meal Planner**.
2. Tap **Generate shopping list**.
3. A new shopping list is created with all ingredients from every recipe in your plan, automatically combined by ingredient.

See [Meal planner](/features/meal-planner) for more details on setting up your weekly plan.

## Collaborating with Pantry Pals

Pantry Pals are collaborators you invite to a specific shopping list. They don't need any special permissions — just a Foryoueats account.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Real-time updates" icon="arrows-rotate">
    When a Pantry Pal checks off an item, you see it update immediately. No more doubling up on groceries.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add items from their end" icon="pen">
    Pantry Pals can add new items to the list, not just check things off. Useful when someone remembers something you missed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Pantry Pals can only access the specific lists you invite them to. They can't see your other lists, your recipes, or your meal plans.
</Note>

## Managing your lists

You can rename or delete any shopping list at any time from the **Shopping Lists** section.
