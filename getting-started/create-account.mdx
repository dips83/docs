title: "Create your free Foryoueats account"
description: "Sign up for Foryoueats in seconds using your email, Google, Facebook, or Apple account."
---

Foryoueats is completely free to use. This page walks you through creating your account and getting oriented once you're in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    Open your browser and navigate to [foryoueats.com/register](https://foryoueats.com/register).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how you want to sign up">
    Pick any of the following sign-up methods:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Email and password" icon="envelope">
        Enter your email address and choose a password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your address.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Continue with Google" icon="google">
        Sign up instantly using your existing Google account — no new password needed.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Continue with Facebook" icon="facebook">
        Use your Facebook account to sign up with a single click.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Continue with Apple" icon="apple">
        Sign up with your Apple ID. Works with Hide My Email if you prefer to keep your address private.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start adding recipes">
    Your account is ready. You can import a recipe from any website by pasting its URL, create a recipe manually, or explore community recipes right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Foryoueats supports both light and dark mode. Switch between them at any time from **Settings → Account → Appearance**.
</Note>
