## Import recipes from your browser

The Foryoueats Chrome extension lets you save recipes from any website without leaving the page. When you find a recipe you want to keep, click the extension icon and it's added to your collection instantly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Chrome extension">
    Search for "Foryoueats" in the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com) and click **Add to Chrome**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pin the extension">
    Click the puzzle-piece icon in your Chrome toolbar, find Foryoueats, and click the pin icon so the extension is always visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save a recipe">
    Navigate to any recipe page and click the Foryoueats extension icon. The recipe imports automatically and appears in your collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Firefox and Safari extensions are coming soon. In the meantime, you can import recipes in those browsers by copying the page URL and using the [URL import tool](/recipes/import-from-url) in the web app.
</Info>

## All your data, everywhere

Your account syncs automatically across all platforms. A recipe you import on your phone appears instantly on the web app and vice versa — no manual syncing required.
