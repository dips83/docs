## Email support

For account issues, bug reports, or any question not covered in the [FAQ](/help/faq), email the team at:

**[support@foryoueats.com](mailto:support@foryoueats.com)**

<Info>
  We typically respond within 1 day.
</Info>

## Share feedback and feature ideas

Foryoueats is a community-driven platform. The best way to shape what gets built next is to share your ideas on the feedback board.

<Card title="Feature request board" icon="lightbulb" href="https://feedback.foryoueats.com">
  Submit ideas, vote on suggestions from other users, and follow along as features move from request to release at feedback.foryoueats.com
</Card>

<Note>
  The team reads every submission on the feedback board. Upvoted ideas move to the top of the roadmap, so your vote genuinely influences what gets built.
</Note>
