Whether you're just getting started or switching from another app, you'll find answers to the most common questions below. If something isn't covered here, reach out to the team at [support@foryoueats.com](mailto:support@foryoueats.com).

## Pricing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does Foryoueats cost?">
    Foryoueats is completely free — no subscription, no hidden fees, no credit card required. You get unlimited recipes, unlimited shopping lists, and a full meal planner at no cost, forever.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Foryoueats really free? Plan To Eat charges a subscription.">
    Yes, Foryoueats is genuinely free. There is no paid tier, no trial period that expires, and no features locked behind a paywall. Everything in the app is available to every user.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recipes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I add ingredients to a shopping list from a recipe?">
    Open any recipe and tap the shopping list button. Your ingredients are added to your active list in one tap — no copying or typing required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import all my recipes from Plan To Eat?">
    Yes. In Plan To Eat, export your recipes as a CSV file, then upload that file using the Foryoueats bulk import tool. See the [bulk import guide](/recipes/bulk-import) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import my recipes from Copy Me That?">
    Yes. Export your recipes from Copy Me That as a ZIP file (which includes your images), then upload the ZIP using the bulk import tool. Your recipe photos come with you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import my recipes from Paprika Recipe Manager 3?">
    Yes. In Paprika, go to **File → Export Recipes → HTML**, then compress the exported folder into a ZIP file and upload it to Foryoueats using the bulk import tool.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import my recipes from Recipe Keeper?">
    Yes. In Recipe Keeper, go to **Settings → Import/Export** and export your recipes as a `.zip` file, then upload that file using the bulk import tool.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & sharing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are my recipes private?">
    You control the privacy of everything you store. Each recipe, cookbook, and shopping list can be set to private (only you), shared with friends, or shared with the entire Foryoueats community. You can change visibility at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data after I import recipes?">
    All imported recipes default to private — only you can see them. You can choose to share individual recipes with friends or with the community whenever you're ready.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I share a cookbook with someone?">
    Yes. You can share any cookbook with specific people or make it public for the community to discover. You can also keep it completely private — the choice is yours.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Pantry Pal?">
    A Pantry Pal is someone you invite to collaborate on a shopping list. They can view and check off items in real time, which makes grocery runs easier when you're shopping together or splitting the list.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account or data?">
    Contact the support team at [support@foryoueats.com](mailto:support@foryoueats.com) and they'll handle your request promptly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Apps & extensions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a mobile app?">
    Yes. Foryoueats is available on the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com) for iOS and on [Google Play](https://play.google.com) for Android. The web app works on any browser as well.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a browser extension?">
    Yes — the Foryoueats Chrome extension is available now. Install it to import any recipe you're viewing with a single click. Firefox and Safari extensions are coming soon.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I suggest a feature?">
    Visit [feedback.foryoueats.com](https://feedback.foryoueats.com) to submit ideas, vote on suggestions from other users, and follow along as features are built. The team actively reviews every submission.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
