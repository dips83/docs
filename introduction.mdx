## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your free account">
    Sign up at [foryoueats.com](https://foryoueats.com/register) using your email, Google, Facebook, or Apple account — no credit card needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first recipe">
    Paste a URL from any recipe website to import it instantly, snap a photo of a cookbook page, or create a recipe from scratch using the recipe editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start cooking">
    Plan meals for the week, generate a shopping list, and discover thousands of community recipes — all in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>
