---
title: "Create a Recipe Manually in Foryoueats"
description: "Build a recipe from scratch using the full recipe editor. Add ingredients, instructions, timing, servings, and choose who can see your recipe."
---

When you want to enter a recipe yourself — from memory, dictation, or your own creation — the manual editor gives you a full set of fields to build a complete, structured recipe.

## Open the editor

Click **Add Recipe** from anywhere in your library, then select **Create manually** to open the recipe editor.

## Fill in the recipe details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Name your recipe">
    Enter a clear, descriptive name in the **Recipe name** field. This is what appears in your library and search results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a description (optional)">
    Write a short description to remind yourself — or tell others — what makes this recipe worth cooking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter ingredients">
    Add each ingredient on its own line. Include the quantity, unit, and ingredient name (for example: *2 cups all-purpose flour*). Foryoueats uses this list to calculate nutritional data automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write the instructions">
    Enter each step in order. Keep each step focused on one action to make the recipe easy to follow while cooking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set timing and servings">
    Fill in **Prep time**, **Cook time**, and **Servings**. Foryoueats displays total time automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your privacy setting">
    Select who can see this recipe before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your recipe">
    Click **Save** to add the recipe to your library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Nutritional data

Foryoueats automatically calculates estimated nutritional information based on the ingredients you enter. Nutritional data appears on the recipe page once the recipe is saved.

<Note>
  Nutritional estimates are calculated from ingredient data and are approximations. They may vary based on specific brands, preparation methods, and portion sizes.
</Note>

## Privacy settings

Control who can see each recipe using the privacy selector in the editor.

| Setting | Who can see it |
|---|---|
| **Private** | Only you |
| **Friends only** | Your Foryoueats connections |
| **Public / Community** | Anyone on Foryoueats |

<Tip>
  You can change a recipe's privacy setting at any time by opening the recipe and clicking **Edit**.
</Tip>
